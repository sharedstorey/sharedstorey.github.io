Dianna & Aiden

Our Story

Several years ago, Aiden packed his bags and moved from New Zealand to Canada on a whim. One night while having dinner at a Toronto diner, he made friends with a few of the servers there – including Dianna’s best friend, Kaelee.


The next day, Kaelee called Dianna and said, “I just met the perfect guy for you.” Dianna laughed, but didn’t think much of this potential match as she was living and working near Washington, D.C. at the time.


For months, Aiden and Dianna heard each other’s names so often that they began to wonder about one another. When they finally met, neither could deny the butterflies they felt. They continued to be in each other’s orbits over the next year until they were both, finally, in the same place, and Dianna admitted that her friend’s instincts may have been right all along.

FAQ

Q: Do you have a gift registry?

Your presence at our wedding is present enough! However, for friends and family who have expressed an interest, we’ve created an online registry with The Bay and Crate and Barrel.

Q: Can I bring a date/family member?

Due to COVID restrictions and venue capacity, our wedding is strictly RSVP-only. We can only accommodate those listed on your invitation.

Q: Are kids welcome?

While we love your little ones, our wedding is an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening. We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home so you can celebrate with us.

Q: Where should I park?

There are several parking garages on both King Street West and Spadina Avenue.

Q: What is the dress code?

The event is black tie optional, so feel free to dust off your spiffiest suit!

Q: Where can I stay if I'd like a hotel near the venue?

We have a room block available at the Hyatt Regency Toronto, 370 King Street W. Please click here to use our exclusive booking link or enter group code G-FASO when booking on Hyatt's website.

Q: What's your wedding hashtag?

Please use #fahelofastorey when posting on social media (special thanks to our friend Aaron for coming up with it).

Q: What are the COVID restrictions?
