Dianna & Aiden

Our Story

Several years ago, Aiden packed his bags and moved from New Zealand to Canada on a whim. One night while having dinner at a Toronto diner, he made friends with some of the servers there – including Dianna’s best friend, Kaelee. Kaelee, a Toronto native, showed Aiden around the city over the next few days and even ended up finding him a place to live.


When she later told Dianna about making friends with one of her customers – a rare occurrence – Kaelee said, “If you weren’t in a relationship with someone right now, I’d tell you I met the perfect guy for you.”


Kaelee pestered both Aiden and Dianna over the next year because she really wanted the two to meet, despite the fact that they were both in relationships with other people. When they finally did meet, Dianna wondered if her friend’s instincts had been right all along...
